# Luminatech Lighting Business Performance Analysis
## Project Overview
This project focuses on analysing large-scale sales, customer, and product data for LuminaTech Lighting using Python. The objective was to transform raw, complex datasets into meaningful insights that can support decision-making across sales strategy, customer retention, and overall business performance. The analysis was conducted as part of a business analytics project, simulating the role of a data analyst working with real-world business data.

# Approach
The project began with extensive data cleaning and preparation, including handling missing values, correcting inconsistencies, standardising variables, and identifying outliers. This step was critical to ensure data integrity before moving into analysis. Once cleaned, exploratory data analysis was performed to better understand the structure of the data and uncover initial patterns and trends.

The analysis then progressed into deeper statistical exploration. Visualisations were created to clearly communicate insights, while two-sample statistical tests were applied to compare key business groups. Multiple linear regression models were also developed to examine the relationship between different variables and profitability, helping identify the main drivers of business performance.

# Insights & Business Value

Through this analysis, key insights were generated around sales trends, customer behaviour, and product performance. The findings highlighted differences in profitability across segments, variations in customer purchasing patterns, and operational factors that influence revenue and costs. These insights were translated into practical, data-driven recommendations aimed at improving decision-making, enhancing customer engagement, and driving business growth.

# Tools & Technologies

The project was completed using Python in a Jupyter Notebook environment. Key libraries included Pandas and NumPy for data manipulation, Matplotlib and Seaborn for visualisation, and statistical libraries such as SciPy and Statsmodels for hypothesis testing and regression analysis.

# Outcome

Overall, this project demonstrates the ability to work with large datasets, apply analytical techniques, and communicate insights in a way that supports real business decisions. It reflects a strong combination of technical skills and business understanding, which are essential for a data analyst role.
